🌍 Why Maintaining ISO 27001 Matters

Beyond just good security hygiene, ISO 27001 certification acts as a global benchmark for trust and credibility. It shows clients, regulators, and partners that your organization values data protection and risk governance.

In many industries, maintaining ISO 27001 is not optional — it’s a prerequisite for:

  • 🏢 Participating in government contracts and public tenders
  • 📦 Onboarding as a vendor with large enterprises
  • 🌐 Establishing credibility in cybersecurity-conscious markets

🔍 What Happens During an ISO 27001 Audit?

Whether it’s your Stage 1 (Readiness Audit) or Stage 2 (Certification Audit), here’s what to expect:

  • ✅ Document Review: Policies, SoA (Statement of Applicability), risk assessments, asset inventories
  • ✅ Process Interviews: Your IT, HR, Security, Legal, and Operations teams may be interviewed
  • ✅ Evidence Checks: Logs, screenshots, training records, incident registers
  • ✅ Physical Checks: Site access control, hardware handling, whiteboard scans

Who’s Involved?

RoleResponsibility
